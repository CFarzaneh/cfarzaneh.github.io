About Us


We provide an Equity crowdfunding community that allows for an innovative society. We look to serve as the platform that will allow the intellectual property of the world to gain funding for their inventions and ideas. With our help, our startups obtain the ability to sustain their invention while gaining the funding for growth.


Our platform allows for unaccredited investors to do their part in promoting a society of innovation and economic growth. Our Startups have been vetted and approved by having the ability to gain sustainability in the market and are serious contenders to compete with innovation.
